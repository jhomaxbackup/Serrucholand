Who are we?

We are a startup, founded at the end of March 2022 and focused on farmers and wholesalers of organic products. Our proposal is to offer them a better promotion and commercialization of their agricultural products, establishing business relationships between farmer and wholesaler in a safe and reliable way taking advantage of virtual media resources.

Mission

Provide better promotion of organic crops to different wholesalers and consumers through a friendly and effective app.

Vision

To be leaders in the agricultural products trade through the use of virtual media available to all.
